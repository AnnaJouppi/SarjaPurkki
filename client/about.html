Welcome to SarjaPurkki!

You can use this app to search for information about different tv shows and cast or crew members. The review section allows you to leave your own review and score about a show.

Following information is available on a show:

  • Name
  • Image
  • Summary
  • Production dates
  • Genre
  • Link to TV Maze information page

Following information is available on cast or crew members:

  • Name
  • Image
  • Birthday and possible death date
  • Birth country
  • Link to TV Maze information page

This app uses TV Maze API.
